Hive Table

A table, inspired by the form of a bee hive. It is a fancy looking interior table.It uses combination of materials - glass and wood; glass and metal; acrylic and wood; acrylic and metal.

Clip Table

The design shows a concept of a table, derived from the form of daily used paper clip. The design includes materials like - wood and glass. The product gives various shelves and spaces to store items as well.

Crystal Lamp

The Dancing Crystal Lamp. A simple table lamp design.Its minimal and modern, modular design. Inspired by the pointed and angular structure of a crystal. The head part gives a look and feel of a crystal.

Bamboo Drone

Bamboo Drone is created using bamboo strips. The objective of this project is to explore bamboo as an alternative resource for constructing the structure of a drone, in this case a quadcopter.

Auditorium seat Cushion Design

The design shows a concept of Auditorium seat upholstery. It is based on amalgamation of exsiting auditorium chairs and car seats.

Tee-Rox

Tee-Rox is a redesign of a playground equipment Sea-Saw. The simple design works on convept of a rocking chair but here it require two kids to work in team inorder to enjoy the ride.

SelFish-Fish Bread

Hypothetical Bread with fish as its main ingredient. The packet in the form of a fish uses sarcasm to depict the main features of the hypothetical bread, also claiming that the bread will be rich in omega 3, with 0 added sugar, cholesterol and Trans Fat.

Virtual Reality Experience of a Car

Virtual reality experience of a car and exploring its subsystem is the goal of this project. Head mounted display and VR box controller is used as a tool to experience the scenario. By gazing through the display, sub parts specifications of the car are displayed for the viewer.

Electric Violin - Fiddle Roll Design

Fiddle role designed out of a hollow bamboo. The design expresses the sense of singularity through its sleek body of one bamboo piece.

Electric Violin - Metallin Design

Metallin is a classic mix of metal and wood. The aluminium section has rounded edges which is comfortable to hold. This is an experiment to design a new way of blending shoulder rest and neck rest.

Denture Sponge Damper

The idea was to design a literal form for product and bring in an element of expression.

Rendered Guitar design

The 3D rendered model of an electric guitar. The form was inspired from the form of cross section of a seashell. The ergonomics of holding the guitar and hand movement while playing was considered while designing the product.
